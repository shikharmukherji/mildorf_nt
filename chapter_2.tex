\section*{Proposition 2.5}

Since $\phi : G\rightarrow H$ and $\varphi : H\rightarrow K$ are both homomorphisms, it is true that

$$\left( \varphi \circ \phi \right)(ab)=\varphi\left(\phi\left(ab\right)\right)=\varphi\left(\phi\left(a\right)\phi\left(b\right)\right)=\varphi\left(\phi\left(a\right)\right)\varphi\left(\phi\left(b\right)\right),$$

for all $a,b\in G$, making $\varphi \circ \phi$ a homomorphism as well.
